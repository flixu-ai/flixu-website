## 1. Data Sovereignty

We respect your data sovereignty. We believe that your data belongs to you.

## 2. Data Processing

- **Ephemeral Processing:** Your inputs are processed ephemerally. We do not store your translation data longer than necessary to provide the service.
- **No Training:** We do not use customer data to train our public models. Your proprietary information remains yours.

## 3. Subprocessors

To provide our service, we utilize enterprise-grade cloud providers with high security standards:

- AWS (Amazon Web Services)
- Hetzner Online GmbH
- Vercel Inc.
- Groq Inc.

## 4. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time.  
If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us at [legal@flixu.ai](mailto:legal@flixu.ai).
